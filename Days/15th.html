Traditional Christmas Foods Around the World

Japan - Fried Chicken

In Japan it has recently become tradition to go to KFC for dinner on Christmas eve. About 3.6 million people do this which means meals need to be reserved about 2 months in advance.

Israel - Latkes

Latkes are fried potaoe pancakes fried in oil. These have been a part of Hanukkah tradition since around the middle ages.

Italy - Panettone

Panettone is a cake filled with candied fruit, nuts, chocolate and raisins. This is now a well known Christmas dish in other parts of the world too.

Sweden - Saffron Buns

Saffron buns are a sweet, yellow pastrie that is often shaped into an S. The yellow colour of course comes form the saffron. It is tradition that they are served to the family by the eldest daughter.

Costa Rica - Tamales

Making the Tamalas is a Christmas tradition in Costa Rica and every familly has their own recipe. The food itself is a corn dough, wrapped in corn husk or a banana leaf and the steamed. The filling may contain pork, beef or chicken.
