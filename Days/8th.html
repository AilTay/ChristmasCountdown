Creative and Cheap Present Ideas

Monochrome box

Do you know the receivers favourite colour? Make a box filled with things of that colour like chocolate, candy, soft drinks, nail varnish or even little toys.

Jar of sweets

Get a cheap jar from the dollar store and fill it with their favourite sweets!

Scrap book

Trying to think of a meaningful present for someone very close? Collect photos of all the good memories you have together and create a scrap book!
