Christmas cake recipe

Ingredients

  1. 1kg mixed dried fruit
  2. zest and juice of 1 orange
  3. zest and juice of 1 lemon
  4. 150ml brandy, plus extra for feeding
  5. 250g softened butter
  6. 200g light soft brown sugar
  7. 175g plain sugar
  8. 100g ground almond
  9. 1/2 tsp baking powder
  10. 2 tsp mixed spice
  11. 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  12. 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  13. 100g flaked almond
  14. 4 large eggs
  15. 1 tsp vanilla extract

Method

  • Put 1kg mixed dried fruit, the zest and juice of 1 orange and 1 lemon, 150ml brandy or other alcohol, 250g softened butter and 200g light, soft brown sugar in a large pan set over a medium heat.
  • Bring to the boil, then lower the heat and simmer for 5 mins. Tip the fruit mixture into a large bowl and leave to cool for 30 mins.
  • Heat oven to 150C/130C fan/gas 2. Line a deep 20cm cake tin with a double layer of baking parchment, then wrap a double layer of newspaper around the outside – tie with string to secure.
  • Add 175g plain flour, 100g ground almonds, ½ tsp baking powder, 2 tsp mixed spice, 1 tsp ground cinnamon, ¼ tsp ground cloves, 100g flaked almonds, 4 large eggs and 1 tsp vanilla extract to the fruit mixture and stir well, making sure there are no pockets of flour.
  • Tip into your prepared tin, level the top with a spatula and bake in the centre of the oven for 2 hrs.
  • Remove the cake from the oven, poke holes in it with a skewer and spoon over 2 tbsp of your chosen alcohol. Leave the cake to cool completely in the tin.
  • To store, peel off the baking parchment, then wrap well in cling film. Feed the cake with 1-2 tbsp alcohol every fortnight, until you ice it.
  • Don’t feed the cake for the final week to give the surface a chance to dry before icing.
