How to make Salt Dough Ornaments:

What you will need:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup table salt
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • Mixing bowl
  • Wooden spoon
  • Parchment paper
  • Rolling Pin
  • Cookie Cutters
  • Straw or toothpick
  • Baking Sheet
  • Glitter, paint and ribbons
  • Polyurethane spray

Method:

  1. Mix the dough, adding the water slowly. Use hands once it is too hard to mis with the spoon.
  2. Kneed the dough until smooth and pliable.
  3. Place the dough between two sheets of parchment then roll to about 1/8" thick.
  4. Using the cookie cutters, cut dough into desired shapes. Then remove the excess.
  5. If you want, add texture to the ornaments.
  6. Using a straw or a toothpick, poke a hole in the top of the ornaments for a ribbon to go through.
  7. Transfer the ornaments and bottom sheet of parchment paper onto a baking tray. Bake in a 300F oven for about an hour. They are done when they feel hard or start to brown.
  8. Decorate with whatever you want!
  9. Seal with the Polyurethane spray.
  10. Put a ribbon through the hole at the top and hange on your tree!
