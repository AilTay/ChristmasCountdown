DIY Christmas Decoration Ideas!

Make your own wreath!

Forage for twigs, holly, pine cones and much more. Then tie together with ribbons and flowers to create your very own wreath! Or, if it's too cold to go outside you can make a wreath out of paper and ribbons.

Make your own Gift wrap! (with a potato stamp)

Get a roll of paper, a potato and some ink. Carve your potato stamp and start creating!

Create a wall decoration out of christmas cards!

Get out all of your old christmas cards and stick them to your wall in whatever shape you want!

Pine cone Tree!

Go outside and pick up as many pinecones as you can to create a cute little pinecone tree. Maybe even put a star ontop.

Hold up your favourite christmas flowers with candy cane!

surround the stems of your beautiful flowers with delicious candy cane to make an amazing decoration.
